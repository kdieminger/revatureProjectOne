About Tuition Reimbursement

Company Name provides reimbursements for university courses, seminars, certifcation preparation classes, certifications, and technical training.
Each employee is able to claim up to $1000.00 in tuition reimbursement per calendar year. The reimbursement coverage for each event is as follows:

Program Reimbursement Coverage
University Courses 80%
Seminars 60%
Certification Preparation Classes 75%
Certification 100%
Technical Training 90%
Other 30%
